Faculties

【Undergraduate】

The Asia Bridge Program (ABP) aims to develop personnel who can act as a bridge between Shizuoka Prefecture and countries in Asia. After enrollment, students take "Intensive Japanese language course" and "ABP foundational courses" in their first semester before studying alongside Japanese students in their desired undergraduate course. Students who pass ABP entrance examinations and enroll in the university are exempt from paying examination fees, enrollment fees, and first-year tuition fees.
Eligibility: Students from India, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, and Myanmar

Please see below for more details.

Graduate Schools

【Master Degree】

As part of the Asia Bridge Program, students can take master's degree courses in subjects which are offered entirely in English: informatics, science, engineering, and agriculture. Students who pass ABP entrance examinations are exempt from paying examination fees, enrollment fees, and first-year tuition fees.
Eligibility: Students from Sri Lanka, Nepal, India, Thailand, Laos, Malaysia, Mongolia, Bangladesh, China, Indonesia, Korea, Singapore, Philippines, Myanmar, Vietnam, and Taiwan
