Hiroshima University International Admissions Office (HU-IAO)

"HU-IAO" is the support page for those who have graduated from universities outside of Japan and wish to study abroad in HU. HU-IAO also runs the system called "Hiroshima University International Admissions Assistant System (HU-IAAS)" which is geared toward prospective students who wish to study at HU especially as a regular graduate student through the Special Selection Examination for International Students or as an international research student (non-degree student).
