Admissions Guide

This page will provide you with necessary information and outline the regulations commonly applied to all international students, regardless of the fields or levels into which they may seek admission.

Please contact us if you have further questions after reading this brochure. Furthermore, each faculty, school, or graduate school has different requirements to evaluate qualifications and different selection procedures. The applicant is advised to inquire at the section of the faculty/school or graduate school concerned with his/her particular field and level of study.
