Admissions

For Those Applying to the Graduate School of Sciences and Technology for Innovation, Tokushima University

Entrance examinations for the doctoral program are usually held in late August each year (depending on enrollment, additional recruitment may be conducted in November or January).
If you live overseas, please first contact your prospective supervisor by email and obtain prior approval to apply.

Doctoral Program: Faculty Introduction
Master’s Program: Faculty Introduction

The application deadline is usually about five weeks before the entrance examination, but it may be shorter for additional recruitment.
In some cases, a preliminary screening may be required, and the screening may take time, so we recommend that you proceed with preparations well in advance.

Selection is based on a comprehensive evaluation that includes an oral examination (the content and method may differ slightly depending on the degree program you are applying to) and a review of the application documents.
If you have any questions regarding application procedures or enrollment procedures, please contact the Academic Affairs Section, Faculty of Integrated Arts and Sciences.

About the Entrance Examination for the Doctoral Program (Department of Science and Technology for Innovation), Graduate School of Sciences and Technology for Innovation

As a graduate school connecting to the Faculty of Integrated Arts and Sciences, the Faculty of Science and Technology, and the Faculty of Bio-Industry—established in FY2016—we reorganized and integrated the “Graduate School of Integrated Arts and Sciences” and the “Graduate School of Advanced Technology and Science” on the Josanjima Campus to newly establish the “Graduate School of Sciences and Technology for Innovation.” By reviewing the vertically segmented education by department, we aim not only to pursue specialized fields but also to cultivate the ability to address international and regional issues such as globalization, energy problems, depopulation and aging, and mental health, and to foster interdisciplinary human resources who can create new value across the domains of science, technology, industry, and society.

How to Request an Application Form

They are available at the service counters of the Academic Affairs Section of the Faculty of Integrated Arts and Sciences, the Academic Affairs Section of the Faculty of Science and Technology, and the Academic Affairs Section of the Faculty of Bioscience and Bioindustry.
If you would like to request the application form by mail, please enclose a return envelope (Kaku-2 size, 33.2 cm × 24.0 cm) with your address clearly written and a 510-yen stamp affixed, and send it to the Academic Affairs Section, Faculty of Integrated Arts and Sciences.
On the outer envelope, please write in red either “Request for Admissions Guidelines for the Graduate School of Sciences and Technology for Innovation (Doctoral Program) Entrance Examination (General Entrance Examination / Special Entrance Examination for Working Adults)” or “Request for Admissions Guidelines for the Graduate School of Sciences and Technology for Innovation (Doctoral Program) Entrance Examination (Special Entrance Examination for International Students).”
