Solving the world’s diverse issues requires a global perspective and the search for and implementation of creative solutions. The University of Tokushima’s doctoral program promotes research that is both locally rooted and globally oriented, and provides a framework that supports the creation of new knowledge from a bird’s eye view that includes your field of specialization and related fields.

Doctoral Program
Doctoral Program

To solve diverse global challenges, we need perspectives beyond specialized fields.

In order to confront the world’s diverse challenges, creative solutions are required. The doctoral program of the Graduate School of Science and Technology aims to nurture individuals with a global perspective who can generate new knowledge by promoting research that transcends the boundaries of their fields of specialization.

Double Degree System.

In addition, we have introduced a double degree system that allows students to obtain both a Japanese degree and a foreign degree in a single program in cooperation with several overseas universities. By utilizing this system, students can deepen their expertise at the University of Tokushima while experiencing different cultures at partner universities abroad and conducting research from a more global perspective.

Learn about the features of the doctoral program.

Our education and research system is characterized by the integration of a wide range of disciplines, including humanities, social and human sciences, science, engineering, and bioresources.
